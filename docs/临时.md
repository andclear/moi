## Design System : 极光玻璃 (Aurora Glass)

### Visual Identity
- **Core Concept**: Future luxury, dynamic gradients, high transparency glassmorphism.

- **Typography**: Sleek sans-serif (Inter/Outfit), wide tracking for headers.
- **Atmosphere**: Fluid, shimmering, multi-layered depth.

### Design Tokens

### Components
